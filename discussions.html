What is Nostr?

Nostr is a decentralized protocol for social communication. Think of it as Twitter, but no single company controls it.

Getting Started:

  1. Get a Nostr Client: Try Damus (iOS), Primal (Web), or Nostrudel
  2. Create Your Identity: Your client will generate a public/private key pair
  3. Get Your npub: This is your public identifier (starts with "npub1...")
  4. Request Access: Send us your npub to join the discussion
